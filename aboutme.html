I was born in NYC and raised in the Bronx.
My teachers noticed a talent for me at an early age.
Art is something that has brought me focus and direction.
I attended LaGuardia High School of Music and art where i honed
my fine arts skills. I delved into oils paints and
acrylics, pottery and printmaking, but Illustration was always
my true love.

I went on to study Graphic design at Katherine Gibbs where I earned
my degree in Visual Communications. I learned the use of many programs such as
Photoshop and Illustrator I gained a good understanding of the field
and went on to delve into another passion, Science.

I currently hold a Full-Time position at
GIA
(The Gemological Institute of America) the worlds foremost authority in
Gems and Gemology. It was here that i was opened to different fields
of science and began my studies as a diamond grader.
I then decided that that was not enough so i enrolled into the BMCC
science program. After a few classes it became extremely apparent
that moving away from my creativity was not in my best interest and
that perhaps following this major might be a waste of time.

I was quickly convinced to go into programming by many of my video game
enthusiast buddies. But programming alone would not be enough for me.
It lacked the creativity i desired. Multimedia programming offered that
and more. So here i am in this class learning to do this in todays world.
