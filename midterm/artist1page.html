ABOUT THE ARTIST

Grace Mateo is a Brooklyn born and based artist. She sought to develop her skills through intensive personal studies and educational institutions. Grace is constantly seeking and discovering new approaches to her work with different mediums. Each piece she treats as if a narrative to her own story. Grace wants to create an exchange and understanding between herself and the viewer, she introduces a space for discovering similitude between us all.Her work arouses discussion on identity and its connection with the body. Through her work she attempt to find acceptance of self, form, and others. She continues to challenge herself, and push for deeper understanding of the human spirit, body, and mind in all aspects of life.
