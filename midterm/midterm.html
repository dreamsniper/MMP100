    • PROJECT ARTLAND

    Welcome to Project Artland created by Steven Taveras a multimedia student at Borough of Manhattan Community College. It is a place where artists can go to be discovered or to discover new talent. A place where you can shop for an artists work or commission them to do a piece, get in contact with them or ask for a collaboration. We feature new artists every month with samples of their work. People from all over the world have been discovered through our site and you could as well. We are always looking for new talent because wiothout you Artland would be nothing. Please leave us a note and tell us how we are doing, or better yet join us, all types of artists and artisans are welcome.
