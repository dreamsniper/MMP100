ABOUT THE ARTIST

Paul Jeter b.1983 Hartford, CT

I am compelled by my ideas to create.
Drawing is the most convenient way to record my inspirations.
A simple sketch can capture a film, a dream,or a be a catalyst
for a comic book. Since I was young, the feeling of drawing
a picture was an escape. It is a pleasure that I intend to
hold onto forever.
