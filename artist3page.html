ABOUT THE ARTIST

Taking inspiration from such artists as Betty Tompkins and Miss Van, Neener's intention is to make the erotic elegant. Neener works with a theme of over-sexualized anthropomorphic imagery. Through the expressive line in the detailed imagery of penetration, masturbation, and genitalia, a new appreciation for the power and poignancy of perversion is conveyed. Strongly anchored in the animal kingdom, Neener's work communicates the truth and importance of embracing o ur inner animal instinct. Her compulsion for the world of DIY encourages a variety of homemade goodies including coloring books, stickers and homemade jewelry.
